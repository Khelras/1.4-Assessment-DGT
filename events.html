Events Happening

There will be various events held at the newly named Critter arena. Some of which will be in relation to animals. We encourge you to bring your pets as we are promoting animal health. We are holding a raffle where people will get extra tickets based on the amount of pets they bring. Another event we will be holding is the pet and owner run. This event will be where petowners and their pets run alongside each other and perform a 5km run. There will also be an animal expo held where you can find various animals there!
